Playing Guide

Considerations before playing

  • Participants play at their own risk and are responsible for their own safety.
  • Participants should only play if they are capable. Please check with your doctor before you begin.

Registration and fees

  • Game fees must be paid prior to the start of the game.
  • Games may be cancelled up to 4 hours beforehand to get a refund.
  • Without 4 hours' notice, participants will forfeit the game and be charged 50% of the game fee.
